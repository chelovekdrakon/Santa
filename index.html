COSMO

Short description

You are a cosmos discoverer on a recently opened planet. Your task is save your live, collect gold discs as many as possible, find key to secret station and get to it.

But one more condition - your space suit has limited an oxygen supply. Don't waste time, you have only 60 items of time.

By the way: if you collect more than 60 discs, you will get one more life!

Rules

You can kill enemies only if you jump on top of them. Be careful with abysses - they are deadly dangerous.

Controls

To move use keys with arrows, and to jump - the longest key on your keyboard (SPACE). To START press to 'ENTER', an to RESTART press to '-'.
